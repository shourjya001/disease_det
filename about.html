ABOUT OUR PROJECT

Our country is a developing country and with the development of technology we have come up with new diseases that never existed earlier. The remedy for these diseases has been found but they aren’t available for each and every section of people, but technology is. The poor people aren’t able to afford the expensive lab tests and then the high fees for consulting a doctor so most of them lose their lives. To overcome this problem, we have come up with a project that will help the people to identify the type of disease they are suffering from and its remedy will also be provided by doctors. We have also added the Do’s and Don’ts for some of the emergency situations like cardiac arrest. This project will also help to determine COVID-19 and provide its remedies so that people can cure themselves by staying at home.
